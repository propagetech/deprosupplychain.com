ABOUT US

De Pro is a private limited company formed by young and dynamic logistics professionals with a combined experience of more than 20 years in supply chain industry.

De Pro is headquartered in Pune and established in the year 2016 with an idea and vision to serve our clients by providing highest quality of professional supply chain and procurement solutions that addresses their business needs.

We bring in our knowledge, experience and expertise from industry and implements best practices resulting into optimal use of resources, infrastructure, and efficiency improvements in day to day operations.

Mission
To be the most trusted, reliable and professional supply chain solution provider firm recognized by our clients for delivering excellence.

Vision
To serve our clients by providing highest quality of professional supply chain solutions that address their business needs.
